About

This page gives a brief explanation about our group and our purpose in creating this website.

Who are we?

We are Group 3 from A04-08-S1-1. The creation of this Website is for our LifeLabs IPBA and for school purposes only.

Purpose

The reason we created this website is to raise awareness about typhoons and share our thoughts on how to mitigate the impacts of typhoons.

Typhoon Odette (Rai)

We were tasked to give information about our assigned typhoon (Odette) and explain how this typhoon positively or negatively affected communities.

Answers

The answers we presented on this website are from our LifeLabs Worksheet, but we summarized it to give the main points of our answers. All of our answers were based of our reasearch which you can locate at the "Sources" page.
