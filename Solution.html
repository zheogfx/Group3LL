Solution

This page includes our proposed solution to mitigate the impacts of typhoons.

Education.

To mitigate the effects of typhoons, communities must be educated about the risks they pose and
how to prepare effectively. This involves understanding warning signals, evacuation protocols, and leveraging advanced technologies like satellite imagery and weather forecasting models for accurate predictions.

Advanced Technology

Our ability to predict typhoon routes with accuracy is improved by utilizing technological development such as satellite imaging and weather forecasting models. Initiatives that are rooted in the community, such as training courses and exercises, are essential for improving preparedness and reaction times.

Cooperation

By working together and continuing to learn, we can strengthen our capacity for recovery and reduce the terrible impacts of these disasters.
